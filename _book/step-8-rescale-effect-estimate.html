8.1 Statistical inference

Since the machine learning algorithms were used only in intermediary steps, rather than estimating our parameter of interest directly, 95% confidence intervals can be calculated directly (Luque-Fernandez et al. (2018)). We will not go into the theory behind extracting the variance.

# EY0_TMLE1 <- mean(Q0W_1_sc)

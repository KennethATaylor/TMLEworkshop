Philosophy

Code-first philosophy is adopted in this workshop.

Pre-requisites

Fluency in R language is required. A general understanding of multiple regression and basic familiarity of machine learning and epidemiological core concepts is expected. Deep understanding of causal inference or advanced statistical inference knowledge is not expected.

Version history

The workshop was first developed for R/Medicine Virtual Conference 2021, August 24th.
